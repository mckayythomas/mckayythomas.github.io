Target Audience

Scenarios

  • Buisness Users

    A small painting company checks the website while planning their outdoor jobs for the week. Easily noting the weather they plan which out door jobsites they can work on through out the week.

    The local Outdoor and Wild-life Volunteer organization has planned a volunteer clean up day for the local parks. After checking the forcast and other local events for the scheduled day they can easily let the volunteers know what clothes to wear and weather gear to bring for the forcast of the event.

  • Personal Users

    A vacationing Family checks the website from their hotel room to see what new events will be taking place during their stay. Noting the weather forcast they also know how to dress accordingly and if they should plan indoor or outdoor activities.

    A local family is planning a hiking trip for their next weekend. Seeing the areas that have the best trails they review the weather forcast and plan their dress accordingly or reschedule for a better time
